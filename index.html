Meet Our Founder

About the Founder

Timothy Feranmi, the visionary founder of Young Codebits Secondary School, embarked on his educational journey with a profound commitment to nurturing young minds. Born and raised in a small town, Feranmi's early life was marked by a relentless curiosity and a passion for learning. His academic excellence earned him a scholarship to a prestigious university, where he pursued a degree in Education and Technology. Upon graduating, Feranmi worked as a teacher in various schools, where he witnessed the transformative power of education. However, he also recognized significant gaps in the traditional educational system, particularly the lack of emphasis on technology and practical skills. Driven by a desire to bridge this gap, he decided to establish his own school. In 2010, Young Codebits Secondary School opened its doors with the mission to provide holistic education that combines academic rigor with practical, real-world skills. Feranmi's innovative curriculum, which integrates technology and coding into the core subjects, quickly garnered attention and praise from educators and parents alike. His approach not only equips students with essential knowledge but also fosters critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. Under Feranmi's leadership, the school has grown into a beacon of educational excellence, producing graduates who excel in higher education and various professional fields. His dedication to continuous improvement and his belief in the potential of every student have been the cornerstones of the school's success. Timothy Feranmi's legacy extends beyond the classroom; he is a mentor, an advocate for educational reform, and a passionate supporter of lifelong learning. His work at Young Codebits Secondary School continues to inspire and shape the future of education.
